About the Studio

Art that lives beyond the frame ★

KAWAII ATELIER is an independent art merch studio translating the masters of Impressionism into wearable, collectible forms — with a kawaii twist. Every tee is screen-printed on heavyweight cotton. Every pin is hard enamel. Every sticker is archival-quality. Every art card is letterpress-printed on cotton rag.
